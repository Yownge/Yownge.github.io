Phantom 3 Series

The phantom 3 series is a great consumer level drone series by DJI. These drones range from $500 to $1000 but they contain many awesome features. Some of which include around 20 minute flight time, 0.5-3.1 mile range depending on the model, and a 12 megapixel camera.
