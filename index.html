What are Drones?

Drones are unmanned aerial vehicles with GPS. There are many types of drones including photography, delivery, search and rescue, military and many more.

 

Photography Drones

Photography drones are usually quadcopters. These quadcopters feature very nice cameras that stream live to the pilot's controller. Photography drones can range from prices of a few hundred dollars to thousands of dollars.

 
 

Emergency Service Drones

Drones have started being used in emergency situations. Drones have many benefits in the emergency service area because their ability to search large areas quickly and gain alternate perspectives. Drones are used in law enforcement, fire fighting, and search and rescue.

 

Land Surveying

Drones are being used in land surveying because they can preprogrammed to survey an area. They can also fly the pattern needed for a highly detailed image relativly quickly. The drones used for land surveying are usually fixed wing drones that look like a airplane.

 
 

Military Drones

The military has many uses for drones. They use drones to monitor areas
